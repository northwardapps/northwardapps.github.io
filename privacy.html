Privacy Policy

App: Granted: US Citizenship Test
Developer: Northward Apps (Christian Kjeldsen)
Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Granted is designed with privacy as a default. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. The app works entirely on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any identifying information. We do not track your usage or behavior. We do not use analytics tools. We do not show ads. We do not sell data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to us: checklist progress, test results, ZIP code for local officials lookup, and your N-400 filing date selection. You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app.

Payments

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. Northward Apps never receives or stores your payment information.

Immigration Information Disclaimer

Granted provides general information only. Nothing in this app constitutes legal advice. Immigration law changes frequently. Always verify requirements at uscis.gov and consult a licensed immigration attorney for your specific situation. Granted is not affiliated with USCIS or any government agency.

Children

Granted is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
